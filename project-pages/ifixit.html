iFixit Fast Fix Project

What is a Fast Fix?

A Fast Fix is a repair guide that is completed as a team. It differs from a common iFixit repair guide where a Fast Fix is something that is basic and does not have many repair steps. As a team we research a topic that could use a basic repair guide. Then as a team we create a proposal that explains what our topic is, the basic repair procedure, and why it could be useful. The repair guide is written on the iFixit website and reviewed by iFixit staff before it is published.

How did I complete this project

For my teams Fast Fix, we chose to do a windshield wiper blade replacement guide on a Audi A4. We wrote a proposal to iFixit staff that explained the main idea, a basic procedure, why the guide is needed, and our teamâ€™s repair experience. After we received a conformation from iFixit to proceed we began to work on the guide. Much of the process involved communicating back and forth with iFixit staff, where we would share our progress and receive critique.

Team collaboration and communication was important for this project, since as a team we needed to pass around ideas and write easy to understand steps, then we needed to present our progress to iFixit writing staff and modify our guide based around their comments.

What did I learn?

Clear and Concise Writing

Providing easy to understand steps that only contain essential information.

Collaborative Reseach / Writing

Working as a group to divide out researching and writing our project guide, as well as communicating with the iFixit team and adapting our work based on feedback.

Communicating Complex Ideas

Organizing a multi-step repair process that has informative headings, basic short form instructions, and clear picutres that all help to communicate the repair process to the reader effectivly.
