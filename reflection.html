Developed Skills

Throughout this course, I have a better understanding of the importance of reader comprehension and how to write and present my ideas more effectively.

Formatting information into clear and easy to understand points helps the reader to better digest and understand the information I am presenting. I think this is the most important skill to have in research writing because there is alot of information to present and it is essential that the reader has a clear understanding of the main points I am trying to convey.

I feel that both my Fast Fix and Accident Report projects helped me to further develop this skill since both require presenting complex ideas and information to the reader in a way that they is easily understandable.

Course Reflection

Many of the skills that I have learned in this course are practical research and writing skills, and because of that I find that I can use many of these skills outside of this course in my academic and professional work.

Discussions I have written on many of the course themes, such as technical writing essentials and writing stratagies, all helped me to gain a betting understanding on important concepts in techincal writing and research and why they are important both in, and outside of this course.

Feedback that I have received on my work from my instructor, peers, and others outside of this course helped me to be more adaptable in being able to adjust and improve my work based to comments I have received.

Challenges

For both projects that I have worked on in this course, they involved working in a group environment. While this made research and writing easier since work could be divided amongst members, there were also issues with communicating and organizing ideas. I worked around this issue by letting each member write their ideas individually in a document and then as a group, revising each others work and merging ideas.

I also found it difficult to understand what information is important when researching for both projects. I plan to continue working on improving this skill in the future by better organising what I plan to say, and then researching and taking ideas that help to futher develop my main ideas.
